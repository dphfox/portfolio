Interval

  • Software
  • Graphics Design
  • UI/UX
  • Music

Physics-based puzzle platformer game built for RDC Game Jam 2021 as a team of five. Developed the branding and UI visual style, and programmed a dynamic music stem system for an evolving orchestral arrangement. Winner of Roblox Innovation award for Most Immersive Environment, RDC 2021 Excellence in Visual Art award, and featured in PCGamesN.

It has received 105.3k total play sessions on Roblox since 9 October 2021, with highly positive reviews at 92.2% upvote/downvote ratio and 12.2k favourites.

Statistics correct as of 14 Jan 2024.
