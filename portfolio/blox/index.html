Blox

Voxel building block game series on Roblox, built to scale to low-end mobile devices. Started as a publicly-playable limited sandbox. Later privately-tested technology expanded capabilities to a full infinitely-generated open world, employing novel meshing techniques to reduce GPU load.

Widely played with 136.5k total play sessions since 3 September 2017. Positively reviewed with 91.8% upvote/downvote ratio on Roblox and 18.8k favourites.

Statistics correct as of 14 Jan 2024.
