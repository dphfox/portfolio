Agnosia

  • Software
  • Graphics Design
  • UI/UX
  • Music

Scenic first-person platformer game built for RDC Game Jam 2020 as a team of three. Developed the branding and UI visual style, and implemented the game's main 'lens switching' mechanic and visual effect. Additionally composed the game's ambient soundtrack and contributed sound effects. Winner of RDC 2020 Excellence in Visual Art award.

It has received 277.3k total play sessions on Roblox since 18 July 2020, with highly positive reviews at 91.8% upvote/downvote ratio and 28k favourites.

Statistics correct as of 14 Jan 2024.
